About Our Company

Committed to Advancing Healthcare Through Quality products

Established in 2011, Topline Surgicals Limited is a Mombasa-based leader in supplying premium medical equipment across East Africa. From orthopedic implants to sterilization systems, we partner with global brands like Ortho-Aid and Shuro to deliver ISO-certified solutions to hospitals, clinics, and homecare providers.

Based at Mombasa Trade Center, we specialize in flexible sales, rental, and leasing programs—trusted by institutions like Coast General Hospital and Aga Khan Hospital Mombasa. Committed to advancing healthcare access, we combine 13+ years of expertise with emergency support and community-focused training initiatives.

What products do you supply?

We supply a wide range of medical equipment including orthopedic implants, hospital furniture, diagnostic and surgical instruments, sterilization systems, and rehabilitation aids.

Do you offer equipment rental or leasing?

Yes, we provide flexible sales, rental, and leasing options for most of our equipment. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

Which brands do you work with?

We partner with leading global brands such as Ortho-Aid, Shuro, and Woosam Medical to ensure quality and reliability in all our products.

Do you provide technical support and training?

Yes, we offer technical support, emergency assistance, and training for healthcare professionals to ensure safe and effective use of our equipment.

How can I request a quote or place an order?

You can request a quote or place an order by calling us, emailing, or using the contact form on our website. Our team will respond promptly to assist you.
